JIN LIN LI

about

I am Jin Lin, a student at Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology with concentrations in design and media arts. Raised with a love for the arts, I aspire to create work that transcends the use of words to communicate and convey emotions. As a student in an interdisciplinary school, I have developed skills and programs in a variety of fields such as coding in Javascript, HTML and CSS, design through Illustrator and media arts in Photoshop, Maya, Adobe Creative Cloud. With competence in a variety of aspects of design and media arts, as well as strong critical thinking, I am able to contribute to several elements of a design project and create cohesive projects as an art director.
